Strawberry Banana Smoothie Recipe

Ingredients for 1 Serving:

  • 1 cup of frozen strawberries
  • 1 cup of frozen sliced bananas
  • Half of a cup of plain or vanilla yogurt
  • Half of a cup of milk (dairy or plant-based)
  • Sugar to taste (start with 1-2 tablespoons and adjust to your preference)

Instructions:

  1. Take the desired amount of frozen sliced bananas from the resealable bag.
  2. Place the frozen strawberries and frozen sliced bananas into a blender.
  3. Add the yogurt, milk, and sugar to the blender.
  4. Blend all the ingredients until smooth and creamy, scraping down the sides of the blender if necessary.
  5. Taste the smoothie and adjust sweetness by adding more sugar if desired.
  6. Blend again briefly to ensure the sugar is fully incorporated.
  7. Pour the smoothie into glasses and serve immediately.

Using sugar as a sweetener allows you to adjust the sweetness level to your preference. Just start with a small amount and add more as needed. Enjoy your delicious Strawberry Banana Smoothie with sugar as a sweetener!
